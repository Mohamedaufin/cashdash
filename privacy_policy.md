# Privacy Policy for CashDash

**Effective Date:** March 25, 2026

**CashDash** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your personal and non-personal information when you use the CashDash mobile application (the "App"). 

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide our expense tracking and budgeting services, we collect the following types of information:

### A. Personal Information You Provide to Us
- **Account Data:** When you register, we collect your Name, Email Address, and Phone Number to create and secure your account.
- **Financial Data:** To function as an expense tracker, the App processes user-generated financial inputs. This includes transaction amounts, categories, merchant names (scanned or entered), and budget limits. **We do not collect banking credentials, complete credit card numbers, or directly interface with your bank accounts.**
- **Customer Support Data:** Information you provide when contacting us via the Help/Notification center within the App.

### B. Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** We may collect device-specific information such as your hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (e.g., Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens) for delivering push notifications.
- **Usage Data & Diagnostics:** App interaction data, crash reports, and performance logs to help us improve app stability.

### C. Device Permissions
The App requests specific device permissions to function correctly. You can revoke these at any time via your device settings:
- **Camera:** We request camera access **strictly** for scanning UPI and payment QR codes. **We use Google ML Kit to process QR codes entirely on-device.** We do not capture, record, store, or transmit any images or video from your camera to our servers or any third parties.
- **Notifications:** To send budget alerts, app updates, and customer support responses.
- **Network Access:** To securely synchronize your data with our cloud databases.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
1. **To Provide Core Features:** Enabling expense tracking, budgeting, transaction history, and QR scanning features.
2. **Account Management & Syncing:** Authenticating users and securely syncing your financial data across multiple devices via your personal account.
3. **Communication:** Sending you necessary administrative notices, budget alerts, and responding to your customer support inquiries.
4. **App Improvement:** Analyzing usage data and crash reports to fix bugs, improve UI/UX, and ensure the stability of the App.
5. **Security & Fraud Prevention:** Detecting and preventing unauthorized access or abuse of the App.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

### A. Third-Party Service Providers
We employ trusted third-party companies to facilitate our service. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and only process data as necessary to perform their services:
- **Google LLC (Firebase Analytics, Authentication, Cloud Messaging, Firestore):** We use Firebase to securely handle user authentication, host our cloud database, and deliver notifications.
- **Google ML Kit:** Used locally on your device for barcode/QR code scanning.

### B. Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, legal process, or in response to a valid request by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

---

## 4. Data Security

The security of your data is critical to us. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:
- Utilizing **Google Firebase** infrastructure, which complies with strict global security standards (ISO 27001, SOC 1/2/3).
- Securing data transmission via HTTPS/TLS encryption.
- We do not store plain-text passwords on your device or our servers.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

### A. Data Retention
We retain your personal and financial data only for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide you the App's services.

### B. Account and Data Deletion (Your Rights)
You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. 
- **In-App Deletion:** You can permanently delete your account directly within the App by navigating to **Profile → Delete Account**.
- **Effect of Deletion:** Completing this action immediately signs you out and triggers a permanent wipe of your Firebase Authentication record, your profile data, and your entire transaction/financial history from our active servers. This action is irreversible.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

CashDash is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete such information from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 7. Spyware and Undisclosed Tracking Policy
In strict compliance with Google Play Store policies:
- We do not secretly track your location.
- We do not enumerate or transmit a list of other applications installed on your device.
- We do not read your SMS messages or access your contacts.
