# Privacy Policy for CashDash

**Last Updated: March 25, 2026**

CashDash ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you sign up or use CashDash, we collect:
- **Name and Email Address**: To identify your account and provide support, via Firebase Authentication.

### Financial Information
The App allows you to track expenses, set budgets, and manage allocations. This data includes:
- **Transaction Data**: Amounts, categories, and timestamps of your expenses.
- **Budget Limits**: Savings and spending goals you set.

### Technical and Usage Data
We collect data through Firebase services:
- **Device Identifiers**: Automatically generated FCM tokens to deliver push notifications.
- **App Activity**: How you interact with the app, used to improve stability and performance.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data to:
- Provide and maintain the App's core budgeting features.
- Synchronize your data across devices via Google Firebase.
- Send important notifications regarding your account or support queries.
- Improve App performance and fix bugs.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
Your data is securely stored using **Google Firebase** (Firestore and Authentication). We use industry-standard encryption and security practices provided by Google Cloud Platform to protect your information.

## 4. Camera Usage
The App requests camera permission **only** to scan UPI QR codes for payment processing. No camera images are stored or transmitted to our servers.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Firebase**: For authentication, database syncing, analytics, and push notifications.
- **Google ML Kit**: For QR code processing — this is done entirely **on-device**. No images are sent to external servers.

## 6. Data Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data directly from within the App (Profile → Delete Account). Upon deletion, your profile and all synced financial records are permanently removed from our servers.

## 7. Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** read your SMS messages.
- We do **not** access your contacts.
- We do **not** track your precise location.
- We do **not** sell your data to any third party.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

## 9. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the **Help** section within the App.
